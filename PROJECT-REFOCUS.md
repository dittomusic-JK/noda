## Key Observations

### What Autonodyne Does RIGHT (Product/Content)

1. **Clear Product Focus**
   - "Autonomy Behaviors" front and center
   - Specific capabilities listed
   - Technical but accessible

2. **Domain Specificity**
   - Works across domains (air, land, sea)
   - Behavior-based approach
   - Platform agnostic messaging

3. **Professional but Approachable**
   - Squarespace site (simple, clean)
   - Not overly designed
   - Content > visual tricks

### What Gambit Does RIGHT (Design/Experience)

1. **Cinematic Immersion**
   - Full-screen visuals
   - AGL counter creates motion
   - Scroll-triggered reveals

2. **Bold Typography**
   - Uppercase, condensed fonts
   - Numbered system (01, 02, 03)
   - Military aesthetic

3. **Visual Hierarchy**
   - ONE idea per screen
   - Massive imagery
   - Minimal text

## Recommended Reset/Refocus

### Core Question: What Is NODA?

Based on your initial content:
- **Orchestration software** for autonomous systems
- **Vendor-agnostic** (like Autonodyne)
- **Zero-friction** integration
- **Multi-domain** coordination

### Simplified Site Structure (Autonodyne-Inspired Clarity)

```
1. HERO
   - Clear value prop (not just "ALGORITHMIC WARFARE")
   - What problem do you solve?
   - For whom?

2. CORE CAPABILITIES (Gambit-style visual)
   - 01: Agentic AI Orchestration
   - 02: Mixed-Fleet Collaboration  
   - 03: Zero-Friction Integration
   - 04: Vendor-Agnostic Architecture
   
3. HOW IT WORKS
   - Technical differentiation
   - Why you vs. Autonodyne?
   - Architecture overview

4. USE CASES
   - Air, land, sea, subsurface
   - Specific scenarios
   - Customer stories

5. TEAM/CREDENTIALS
   - NASA, MIT, DARPA heritage
   - Brief, credible

6. CTA
   - Demo request
   - Contact
```

### What to CUT/Simplify

**Remove:**
- ❌ Trust Indicators section (redundant with credentials)
- ❌ Canyon Metrics with Three.js (cool but distracting)
- ❌ Dashboard Hero section (what is this?)
- ❌ Statement Section ("coordinate like teams" - too abstract)
- ❌ Multiple overlapping capabilities sections
- ❌ Heavy tactical overlays (too gamified)

**Keep:**
- ✅ Hero (drone video + headline)
- ✅ Core Capabilities (Gambit-style 01/02/03/04)
- ✅ Why NODA (if simplified)
- ✅ Final CTA

## Content Strategy Shift

### Current Problem
- Leading with "ALGORITHMIC WARFARE" is bold but vague
- No clear explanation of WHAT the product IS
- Gambit-inspired visuals without Gambit's clarity

### Recommended Approach (Autonodyne-Inspired)

**Hero Should Say:**
```
AUTONOMOUS ORCHESTRATION PLATFORM
Coordinate multi-domain UxVs without platform modifications

[Shows what it does in 10 seconds]
```

**Or (More Direct):**
```
ZERO-FRICTION AUTONOMOUS TEAMING
Vendor-agnostic orchestration for air, land, sea, and subsurface systems

[Visual: Multiple UxVs coordinating]
```

### Key Messages (Autonodyne Parity)

| Autonodyne Says | NODA Should Say |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| "Autonomous behaviors" | "Autonomous orchestration" |
| "Numerous unmanned vehicles" | "Multi-domain UxVs" |
| "Different domains" | "Air, land, sea, subsurface" |
| Simple, clear | Simple, clear + Gambit visual polish |
